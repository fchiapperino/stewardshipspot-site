About Frank

Clear language. A steady plan. Next steps your leaders can actually execute.

Frank Chiapperino is a Senior Stewardship Consultant with INJOY Stewardship. He previously served as a Senior Pastor and Executive Pastor, bringing 25 years of ministry experience to the work.

He helps churches fund their vision and projects with clarity—without gimmicks, guilt, or awkward pressure.

Stewardship Spot exists to give pastors and leaders practical tools for real-world needs like deferred maintenance, capital projects, and major-gift conversations—rooted in healthy leadership and faithful stewardship.

  • Calm & Pastoral: helps you talk about money and mission with integrity and confidence.
  • Practical & Clear: provides frameworks, scripts, and next steps your team can actually use.
  • Strategy + Data: brings financial insight and a proven process to move from concern to action.

How we help

Simple support, tailored to your situation.

1) Clarify the need

What is the real problem? What is urgent vs. important? What does “healthy” look like?

2) Build the message

Concrete language, compelling mission connection, consistent cadence, and clear consequences.

3) Invite participation

A plan for the whole church plus a respectful pathway for major-gift conversations.
